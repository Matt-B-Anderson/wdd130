Oarsome-Rafting

At Oarsome-Rafting we try our best to put the guest first. This is insured by our vigorous training process for all of our staff.

History

Established in 1948 in Salmon Idaho. Currently found in every state with rivers containing class 3 and above rapids.

We started small with 5 people who loved to raft. Our first "raft" was 5 tractor tires tied together.
