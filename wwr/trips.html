Available Trips
Rivers Camping Rafting
Not sure if you want to commit to rafting or camping, but want to spend some time on the river? Join us on a slow paced river float with a nice fine dinning experience. So you want to come to the river, but you still don't want to raft? We have full camping facilities available. You can rent a yurt, a campsite, or a designated hammock spot. We have a full-service restroom at each location. We have 3 different levels of rafting: never been, been a few times, I can navigate myself. All of which include a guide, whether wanted or not. We provide all the necessary gear. No matter which level you choose plan to spend a minimun of 3 hours on the river.

Never been raftn', no problem! Come once with us and you might never leave the river...
